AI Problem (click)
AI systems are everywhere now, but many are not clear or transparent. People may not know when they are talking to AI, and some systems can give biased or incorrect results.
Main Rules
  • AI must clearly say it is AI
  • Important decisions must be explained
  • Harmful or misleading content is limited
  • Regular testing is required
Who Benefits
Students, workers, and everyday users benefit from safer AI. Businesses also gain trust from customers.
Criticism
Some believe the law could slow down innovation or be hard to enforce. Others think it adds too many rules for companies.
