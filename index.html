AI Transparency and Safety Act

What AI Problem It Solves

Artificial Intelligence is growing fast, but many systems are not transparent. People often don’t know when they are interacting with AI, and some systems can spread misinformation or make biased decisions.

Main Rules of the Law

  • All AI systems must clearly identify themselves as AI
  • Companies must explain how their AI makes important decisions
  • Strict limits on harmful or misleading AI content
  • Regular safety testing and audits

Who Benefits

This law protects everyday users, students, workers, and businesses by making AI safer and more trustworthy. It also helps governments ensure fair use of technology.

Possible Criticisms

Some people argue that these rules could slow down innovation or make it harder for companies to develop new AI technologies. Others believe enforcement may be difficult.

Why Voters Should Support It

Supporting this law means supporting safer technology, better protection of personal rights, and a more transparent digital future. It ensures AI works for people—not against them.
