AI Transparency and Safety Act

Building a safer, smarter future with responsible AI

⚠️ AI Problem

AI is powerful but often invisible. Users don't know when AI is making decisions, which can lead to bias, misinformation, and lack of accountability.

📜 Main Rules

  • AI must identify itself
  • Explain important decisions
  • Limit harmful content
  • Mandatory safety audits

✅ Who Benefits

  • Students
  • Workers
  • Consumers
  • Businesses

⚖️ Criticism

Some say it slows innovation and is hard to enforce, but safety and trust are more important long-term.
