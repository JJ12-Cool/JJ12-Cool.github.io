AI Transparency and Safety Act

Making AI safer, fairer, and more accountable

What AI Problem It Solves

AI is becoming part of daily life—chatbots, recommendation systems, and automated decisions. However, many systems lack transparency. Users often don’t know:

  • If they are talking to AI or a human
  • How decisions are made (like job screening)
  • Whether bias or misinformation is involved

This law addresses these risks directly.

Main Rules of the Law

  • AI Disclosure: Systems must clearly identify themselves
  • Explainability: Companies must explain major decisions
  • Content Limits: Harmful or misleading outputs are restricted
  • Audits: Regular safety checks are required
  • User Rights: People can question or appeal AI decisions

Who Benefits

This law benefits:

  • Students – protection from misinformation
  • Workers – fairer hiring systems
  • Consumers – safer online experiences
  • Businesses – increased trust from users

Possible Criticisms

Critics argue:

  • It may slow innovation
  • Compliance could be expensive
  • Enforcement might be difficult globally

However, supporters argue safety and trust outweigh these concerns.

Why Voters Should Support It

This law promotes transparency, fairness, and accountability in AI. It ensures technology serves people responsibly.
