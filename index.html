AI Transparency and Safety Act

A simple proposal for safer AI systems

Problem Rules Benefits Criticism Support

AI Problem

AI is used in many areas, but people often don’t know how it works. This can lead to unfair decisions, bias, and misinformation.

Main Rules

  • AI must clearly identify itself
  • Important decisions must be explained
  • Limit harmful or misleading content
  • Regular testing for safety

Who Benefits

This law helps students, workers, and everyday users by making AI more trustworthy. Businesses also benefit from increased public confidence.

Criticism

Some believe the law may slow innovation or be difficult to enforce. Others argue the benefits outweigh these concerns.
